Bio
I am a third-year student of School of Statistics, Renmin University of China. Now I am into the causal inference field, including missing data, truncation by death, instrumental variable and causal discovery.
I am also interested in machine learning and artificial intelligence. I have an internship in Meituan as an algorithm developer in 2023. My undergraduate thesis in Nanjing University is about Multi Armed Bandits (Reinforcement Learning). And I have participated in a national level project about screening information from website. So, I am also curious about the combination of causal inference and machine learning, for the great power it may have!
I was born in 1999, in Yantai, China, a gorgeous place well known for its delicious apples. In my spare time, I like to read books, all kinds of books, such as history, fiction, etc.
